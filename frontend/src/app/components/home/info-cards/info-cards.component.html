Mutual Fund

A pooled investment vehicle that collects money from many investors to purchase a diversified portfolio of stocks, bonds, or other securities — professionally managed on your behalf.

Beta

Measures a fund's systematic risk relative to the overall market. A beta greater than 1 means more volatile than the market; less than 1 means less volatile.

Return Rate

The expected annual return calculated using the CAPM model, which factors in the risk-free rate, the fund's beta, and the expected market return.
